1.1.2 Quickstart

Assuming you have satisfied the dependencies and downloaded the latest ThesisPUC.zip (see Obtaining and Installing ThesisPUC), then:

  • create a folder where you plan to develop your thesis
  • drop the ThesisPUC.zip archive in it
  • unzip ThesisPUC.zip in that folder

You should see the following list of files and directories: 

atbeginend.sty
doc/
examples/
NEWS		[ new, since version 1.0.3
puc.png
ThesisPUC.bst
ThesisPUC.cls
ThesisPUC.zip

If you wish to, you may now remove the ThesisPUC.zip.

It is highly recommended for users who upgrade from a previous ThesisPUC version to carefully read the NEWS file, and apply the necessary changes when appicable.
